# ChronoGuard

ChronoGuard is an intelligent scheduling security assistant designed to proactively manage calendar conflicts, extract and track professional commitments, and synthesize analytical pre-meeting briefs. By integrating deeply with Google Workspace services and utilizing advanced semantic analysis, the system acts as an autonomous guardian for your schedule.

---

## Core Functions

### 1. Smart Commitment & Risk Engine
* **Commitment Extraction**: Automatically extracts commitments, action items, and agreements from professional communications.
* **Risk Score Assessment**: Analyzes schedule density, historical deadlines, and task load to generate a real-time risk rating for each commitment.
* **Proactive Warning System**: Identifies high-risk commitments nearing their target deadlines and provides actionable warnings.

### 2. Vertex AI Pre-Meeting Briefings
* **Pre-Meeting Intel**: Generates context-aware briefings for upcoming Google Calendar events.
* **Strategic Summaries**: Gathers attendee information, key talking points, and preceding context to help prepare for critical syncs.
* **Multi-Channel Delivery**: Delivers briefs via the in-app interactive viewer or drafts summaries directly to your email inbox.

### 3. Google Workspace Sync
* **Calendar Synchronization**: Connects to Google Calendar to synchronize scheduled events, upcoming meetings, and focused working blocks.
* **Gmail action-item Parsing**: Parses email threads for implied action items, deadlines, or requested deliverables.
* **Unified Status Dashboard**: Aggregates calendar state, pending emails, and commitments into a single clear interface.

### 4. Interactive Voice Control
* **Natural Language Queries**: Supports voice-driven search, status updates, and view switches.
* **Hands-Free Dashboard Management**: Execute commands to generate meeting briefs, filter high-priority risks, or request status reports.

### 5. Automated Remediation Workflows
* **Communication Drafts**: Generates professional shift request emails and timeline adjustments automatically when schedule conflicts arise.
* **Calendar Time-Blocking**: Secures recommended focus blocks on your calendar to protect active working hours for critical deliverables.

---

## Architectural and UI Design

The ChronoGuard interface is structured to prioritize cognitive clarity and rapid focus:
* **Structured Information Density**: Uses precise spatial layout and typography to display high-priority alerts without causing sensory fatigue.
* **Swiss Modern Aesthetic**: Employs a clean, high-contrast typography scheme paired with space-efficient tabs and custom action sheets.
* **Context-Driven Color Indicators**: Maps urgency, security flags, and schedule status to low-saturation colors to maintain a professional, calm workspace environment.
